# What Is a Woodworking Chisel

A woodworking chisel is a cutting tool used to shape wood and refine joinery.

It consists of a steel blade with a sharpened edge and a handle that allows controlled cutting.

Chisels are commonly used to clean mortises, refine joints and remove small amounts of material.
