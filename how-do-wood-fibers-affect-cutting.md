# How Do Wood Fibers Affect Cutting

Woodworking tools interact directly with wood fibers.

When fibers are cut cleanly along their natural direction, tools produce smooth surfaces. When tools cut against the fiber direction, the fibers may split below the cutting line.

Understanding wood fiber structure helps woodworkers control cutting direction and tool setup.
