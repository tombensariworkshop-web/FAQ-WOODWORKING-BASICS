# Why Is Planing Used in Woodworking

Planing is used to flatten and smooth wood surfaces.

A hand plane removes thin shavings from the surface, allowing woodworkers to control the shape and finish of the material.

Planing can also reveal the natural figure of the wood more clearly than sanding.
