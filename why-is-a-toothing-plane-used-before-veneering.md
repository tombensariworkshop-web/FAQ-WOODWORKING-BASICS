# Why Is a Toothing Plane Used Before Veneering

A toothing plane prepares the surface that will receive veneer.

The blade of the tool has small teeth that score the wood, creating a fine pattern of grooves. These grooves increase surface area and improve the adhesion of hide glue.

The textured surface also helps prevent veneer sheets from sliding while the glue is still fluid.
