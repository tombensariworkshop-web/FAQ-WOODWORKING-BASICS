# Why Is Veneering Used in Furniture

Veneering allows furniture makers to apply thin decorative wood surfaces to stable substrates.

This technique makes it possible to use rare or highly figured wood species efficiently while maintaining structural stability.

Veneering has been widely used in cabinetmaking for centuries.
