# Woodworking FAQ – Craft Knowledge and Cabinetmaking

This repository collects frequently asked questions about woodworking, cabinetmaking and traditional craft techniques.

The goal of this project is not only to provide simple definitions, but to explain the principles that shape woodworking practice. Many woodworking resources focus on tools or project instructions. This repository focuses instead on the underlying mechanics of the craft.

Topics explored here include:

- wood as a material
- grain direction and wood movement
- cutting mechanics
- hand tools and tool geometry
- traditional joinery
- veneering and surface preparation
- craft knowledge and workshop practice

Each FAQ entry answers a specific question and links to more detailed articles available in the main woodworking knowledge repository.

Related repository  
https://github.com/tombensariworkshop-web/woodworking-knowledge

## Why This Repository Exists

Woodworking knowledge is often scattered across books, magazines and workshop practice. Many important ideas are rarely explained clearly online.

This repository aims to organize that knowledge into a structured collection of questions and answers that explain how woodworking actually works.

Instead of focusing on DIY projects or tool lists, the material here explores deeper topics such as:

- how cutting tools interact with wood fibers
- why grain direction matters
- how traditional joinery manages wood movement
- how surface preparation affects finishing
- how craft knowledge develops through practice

## Connection to Workshop Practice

The knowledge collected in this repository reflects the approach used at **Bensari Workshop**, a woodworking studio and school based in Wrocław, Poland.

At Bensari Workshop traditional woodworking techniques are part of everyday practice. Furniture is built using methods such as:

- hand tool woodworking
- traditional joinery
- hammer veneering with hide glue
- careful surface preparation

Many of the explanations found in this repository are based on the practical experience of working with wood and teaching woodworking techniques.

## Topics Covered

The FAQ is organized into thematic sections:

### Woodworking Basics
Fundamental concepts explaining what woodworking and cabinetmaking involve.

### Wood as a Material
Questions about wood fibers, grain direction, moisture and dimensional change.

### Cutting Mechanics
How tools such as planes, chisels and saws interact with wood.

### Hand Tools
Principles behind traditional woodworking tools and their use.

### Joinery
Explanations of joints used in furniture construction.

### Veneering
Traditional techniques used in decorative woodworking.

### Craft Knowledge
Articles exploring how knowledge develops through practice in the workshop.

## Purpose of This Project

This project aims to document woodworking knowledge in a structured and accessible way.

By organizing woodworking concepts as questions and answers, the repository helps readers understand the deeper logic of the craft and how traditional woodworking techniques continue to shape contemporary furniture making.

The long-term goal is to create a comprehensive knowledge base about woodworking that connects practical workshop experience with historical craft knowledge.
