# Why Are Dovetail Joints Used in Drawers

Dovetail joints are commonly used in drawers because their interlocking shape resists pulling forces.

This makes the joint particularly strong when drawers are opened and closed repeatedly.

Dovetails have been widely used in cabinetmaking for centuries.
