# How Do Woodworking Skills Develop

Woodworking skills develop through repeated interaction with tools, materials and processes.

While theoretical knowledge is useful, many aspects of the craft can only be understood through direct practice.

Over time, woodworkers learn to recognize grain direction, control cutting tools and anticipate how wood behaves.
