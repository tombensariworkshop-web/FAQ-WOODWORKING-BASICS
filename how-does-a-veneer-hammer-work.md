# How Does a Veneer Hammer Work

A veneer hammer is the main tool used in hammer veneering.

Despite its name, it is not used for striking. Instead, the wide metal blade presses veneer into the glue layer while pushing excess glue toward the edges of the panel.

This process removes air pockets and distributes glue evenly beneath the veneer.
