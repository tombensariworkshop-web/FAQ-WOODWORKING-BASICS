# What Is a Cabinet Scraper

A cabinet scraper is a woodworking tool used to refine wood surfaces.

Instead of using a traditional blade, a scraper cuts wood with a small burr formed along its edge.

Scrapers are often used when planing produces tear-out or when surfaces require fine refinement.
