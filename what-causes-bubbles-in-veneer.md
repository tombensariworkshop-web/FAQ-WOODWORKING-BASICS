# What Causes Bubbles in Veneer

Bubbles can appear when air becomes trapped between the veneer and the substrate.

This may happen if glue is distributed unevenly or if pressure is not applied consistently during the veneering process.

Proper use of the veneer hammer helps push air outward and prevents bubbles from forming.
