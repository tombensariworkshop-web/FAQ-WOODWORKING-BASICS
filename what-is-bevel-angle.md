# What Is Bevel Angle

The bevel angle is the angle formed between the cutting edge of a blade and the surface being cut.

In woodworking tools such as chisels and plane irons, the bevel angle influences how the blade interacts with wood fibers.

Different bevel angles can improve cutting efficiency or help control tear-out.
